1. NEWS COVER IMAGES
News cards now use reliable local logistics cover photographs. Google News logos, blank covers and escaped HTML such as &lt;a href= are removed.

2. FEATURED LISTINGS

Chat no longer requires a visible login, account creation or Supabase Anonymous Sign-Ins. It uses private device keys. A buyer can open and send a message immediately. The listing poster sees and replies to it from the device used to publish the listing.
