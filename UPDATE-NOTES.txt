SECURITY NOTE
Messages are protected by Supabase Row Level Security, HTTPS/TLS in transit and the database/storage platform's encryption at rest. This is not advertised as end-to-end encryption. A dedicated cryptographic/security audit is required before making an end-to-end encryption claim.
